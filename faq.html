How do I book an appointment at Ruksana Medical Trust?

You can book through our website's appointment page, by calling 1800-000-0000, via WhatsApp at +91-9000-000-000, or by visiting the hospital directly. Online booking is available 24/7 and confirmation is sent within 2 hours.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 4 hours before the scheduled time by calling our helpline or through WhatsApp. For online appointments, you'll receive a confirmation link which also provides a reschedule option.

Does Ruksana Medical Trust offer emergency services?

Yes, our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We have a dedicated trauma centre, ICU and critical care unit. Our emergency number is 1800-000-0000 (toll free).

Does the hospital have pharmacy and diagnostic services?

Yes, we have a 24/7 in-house pharmacy and our diagnostic centre offers comprehensive lab tests, X-ray, MRI (3T), CT scan, PET-CT, ultrasound, echocardiography and more. Home sample collection is also available between 6 AM and 10 AM.

Which insurance plans are accepted?

We accept all major insurance providers: Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, ICICI Lombard, New India Assurance, United India Insurance, IFFCO Tokyo and more. We also accept Ayushman Bharat PMJAY and CGHS. Contact our insurance desk for specific queries.

How does the cashless insurance claim process work?

For cashless treatment: (1) Present your insurance card at admission, (2) Our insurance desk will obtain pre-authorization from your insurer, (3) Treatment proceeds after approval. For planned procedures, we recommend sending the request 48 hours in advance. Our team handles all paperwork on your behalf.

What are the visiting hours for inpatients?

General visiting: 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM. ICU visiting: 15 minutes twice daily at 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM, one visitor at a time. Please carry a valid photo ID. Children under 12 are not permitted in ICU areas.

Is there parking available at the hospital?

Yes, we have a multi-level covered parking facility with 500+ spaces. First 2 hours are free for OPD patients. Valet parking is available at the main entrance (₹100). Dedicated spaces are provided for differently-abled visitors near the entrance.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, all major debit/credit cards, UPI (GooglePay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and EMI options through partner banks. For large bills, EMI without cost is available for up to 12 months. Insurance and Ayushman Bharat are also accepted.

Can I get a detailed medical bill or discharge summary?

Yes, detailed itemized bills and discharge summaries are provided at the time of discharge. Digital copies are emailed to your registered address. For additional copies or clarifications, contact our billing department at billing@ruksanamedical.com.

Does Ruksana Medical Trust offer telehealth or online consultations?

Yes, we offer teleconsultation services for follow-ups and routine consultations with select specialists. To book a teleconsultation, select "Teleconsultation" as the appointment type in the booking form or call our helpline. Video sessions are conducted via a secure encrypted platform.

Do I need a referral letter to see a specialist?

No referral is required for direct specialist consultations at Ruksana Medical Trust. You can book directly with any specialist of your choice. However, if you have been referred by a general physician, please bring the referral letter and any previous reports for a more comprehensive consultation.
