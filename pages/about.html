Our Story

Built on a Foundation of Trust & Care

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Ruksana Ahmed, a visionary physician with a dream to bring world-class healthcare to every family, Ruksana Medical Trust began as a 50-bed facility in Hyderabad. Today, it has grown into a 500-bed multi-speciality hospital with over 200 doctors, 1,000+ staff and 25 departments.

Over the past 25 years, we have treated over 50,000 patients, performed more than 10,000 surgeries and earned 148+ national and international awards for clinical excellence, patient safety and hospital management.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, accessible and technologically advanced healthcare to every individual — regardless of socio-economic background — while maintaining the highest standards of clinical excellence, patient safety and ethical practice.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted multi-speciality healthcare institution, recognized globally for innovation, patient outcomes and a culture of continuous learning — transforming lives and communities through the power of healing.

Our Journey

25 Years of Achievements

Key milestones that mark our journey of excellence in healthcare.

1999

Founded

Dr. Ruksana Ahmed establishes Ruksana Medical Trust as a 50-bed general hospital in Hyderabad.

2004

Multi-Speciality Expansion

Expanded to 200 beds with dedicated Cardiology, Neurology and Orthopedics departments.

2009

NABH Accreditation

Received National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers certification — the first in the region.

2014

Cancer Centre Launch

Opened a dedicated Comprehensive Cancer Centre with chemotherapy, radiation and palliative care.

2018

Robotic Surgery

Introduced India's most advanced robotic surgery system — performing 500+ procedures in the first year.

2024

500-Bed Facility

Completed the ₹200 crore expansion to a 500-bed facility with 25 speciality departments and a research centre.
