FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hospital, services and appointment process.

How do I book an appointment at Ruksana Medical Trust?

You can book an appointment through our website's appointment page, by calling our helpline 1800-000-0000, via WhatsApp, or by visiting the hospital directly. Online booking is available 24/7 and you'll receive a confirmation call within 2 hours.

Does Ruksana Medical Trust offer emergency services?

Yes, our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. We have a dedicated trauma centre, ICU and critical care unit staffed by experienced emergency medicine specialists. Call 1800-000-0000 for emergencies.

Which insurance plans are accepted at Ruksana Medical Trust?

We accept all major insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, ICICI Lombard, New India Assurance, United India Insurance, and more. We also accept Ayushman Bharat PMJAY. Please contact our billing department for specific queries about your coverage.

What are the visiting hours for inpatients?

General visiting hours are 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM. ICU visiting is limited to 15 minutes twice daily (10:00 AM and 5:00 PM). One visitor is permitted at a time in ICU areas. Please carry a valid ID for entry.

Does the hospital have pharmacy and diagnostic services?

Yes, we have a 24/7 in-house pharmacy stocked with all essential medications. Our diagnostic centre offers comprehensive lab tests, X-ray, MRI, CT scan, ultrasound, PET-CT, echocardiography, and more. Home sample collection is also available.

Is there parking available at the hospital?

Yes, we have a multi-level covered parking facility with 500+ spaces. The first 2 hours are complimentary for OPD patients. Valet parking is available at the main entrance. We also provide dedicated parking for differently-abled visitors.
